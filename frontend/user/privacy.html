Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect only what is needed to deliver secure financial services.

  • Identity details such as name, email, phone, BVN, or NIN.
  • Account activity including transfers, bill payments, and balances.
  • Device and session data for fraud prevention.
  • Support conversations and service feedback.

How We Use Data

We use data to operate, protect, and improve the platform.

  • Verify identity, enable transfers, and issue dedicated accounts.
  • Detect fraud and enforce security policies.
  • Provide receipts, alerts, and account updates.
  • Enhance product experience and customer support.

Sharing & Disclosure

We share data only when necessary and lawful.

  • With payment partners and service providers for processing.
  • With regulators or authorities when required by law.
  • With auditors or advisors under confidentiality obligations.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Security & Retention

Security controls protect your data throughout its lifecycle.

  • Encryption for data in transit and at rest.
  • Access controls, monitoring, and incident response.
  • Retention aligned with regulatory requirements.
  • Secure deletion when retention is no longer required.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics to keep sessions secure.

  • Session cookies keep you signed in securely.
  • Analytics help us improve stability and performance.
  • You can clear cookies via your browser settings.
  • We do not use cookies for third-party advertising.

Your Rights

You have control over your personal information.

  • Request access to your profile data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion when legally permitted.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.
