Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about transfers, bills, security, and account access.

How do I secure my account?

Use a strong password, enable device verification, and keep your PIN private.

  • Never share OTPs, passwords, or your transaction PIN.
  • Enable biometric approvals when available.
  • Review your login history regularly.
Why is my transfer pending?

Transfers can be delayed due to bank processing windows or compliance checks.

  • Bank settlements can take additional time on weekends.
  • High-value transfers may be reviewed for security.
  • Check Activity for real-time status updates.
How do bill payments work?

Select a provider, confirm the amount, and we process instantly with a receipt.

  • We validate the account or meter number before payment.
  • Receipts are issued immediately after confirmation.
  • Failed payments are reversed where applicable.
How do I increase my limits?

Complete KYC in Settings to unlock higher transfer and bill limits.

  • Submit accurate BVN/NIN and address details.
  • Approval timelines vary by verification level.
  • Track KYC progress from the Settings page.
Can I receive money with QR?

Yes, you can share your receive QR or create a request QR for any amount.

  • Share your QR directly from the Receive page.
  • Create request QR codes for invoices or reminders.
  • All QR transfers post directly to your wallet.
