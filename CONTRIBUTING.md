# Contributing (Private)

GLY VTU is a private project. Contributions are **not** accepted without
explicit permission from the owner.

## Request Access
If you want to contribute:
1. Obtain written permission from the owner.
2. Share your intended scope of work.
3. Wait for approval before making changes.

## Rules (When Approved)
- Follow the security policy in `SECURITY.md`.
- Do not introduce credentials or secrets in commits.
- Keep changes focused and documented.
- All changes must be reviewed by the owner.
